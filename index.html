Rasec Marc

Rasec Marc is a Filipino-American singer/songwriter, producer, and composer. Fusing emotional lyricism with colorful pop-oriented instrumentation, his music creates a space for listeners to feel understood and empowered. From the heartstring tugs of Hurts to the infectious, love-soaked Conclusions, each song offers both a unique message to connect with and a beautiful world to get lost in.

I created Rasec's website using html, vanilla CSS, and jquery.

Pokédex

Being a Pokémon fan since childhood, I decided to create a simple Pokédex that displays Pokémons from gen 1 to gen 5 . When one of the Pokémon cards is clicked, it displays that Pokémon with some of its in-game stats. Gotta catch ém all!

I created this using reactjs, Tailwind CSS, and poke api.
